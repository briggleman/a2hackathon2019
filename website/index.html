Culinary Coin

We Provide Inspiring and Educational Culinary Experiences to High School Students in Washtenaw County

Are you a high school student that loves food? We would love to show you what Washtenaw County’s food community has to offer! Our mission is to inspire, teach, and help the next generation of chefs, food entrepreneurs, and service industry professionals in Washtenaw County. It’s free to apply for our culinary program and it’s also free to participate.
